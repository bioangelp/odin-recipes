Zombie Cake

Description:

This delicius zombie cake is perfect to feed your zombies

it contains all the nutriens a zombie needs to continue dead

please give yourself a favor and try out this recipe, you

not ganna regret it.

General information

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 30 mins
  • Total Time: 40 mins
  • Servings: 12
  • Yield: 1 9-inch square cake

Incredians

  • 2 kilograms of human meat

  • 1 cup white sugar

  • ½ cup unsalted butter

  • 2 large eggs

  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

  • 1 ¾ teaspoons baking powder

  • 2 cup milk

Steps

  1. Mix the 2 kilograms of human meat, 1 cup white sugar, ½ cup unsalted butter, 2 large eggs, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour, 1 ¾ teaspoons baking powder, and 2 cups milk in a large mixing bowl until well combined.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9-inch square cake pan.
  3. Pour the cake batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Remove the cake from the oven and allow it to cool completely on a wire rack.
  6. Once cooled, slice and serve to your zombie guests, ensuring they get all the nutrients they need to continue being dead.
