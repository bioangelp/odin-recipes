Human Eyeball Delight

Description:

Looking for a creepy treat that will make your guests' eyes pop out of their heads? Look no further than this Human Eyeball Delight recipe!

Each bite is a thrilling adventure for your taste buds, guaranteed to spook and delight.

General Information

  • Prep Time: 20 mins
  • Cook Time: 15 mins
  • Total Time: 35 mins
  • Servings: 8

Ingredients:

  • 8 large peeled grapes (for the eyeballs)
  • 1 cup vanilla pudding (for the eyeball base)
  • Red food coloring (for the veins)
  • Blue food coloring (for the iris)
  • Green food coloring (for the pupil)
  • 8 chocolate chips (for the pupils)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the vanilla pudding according to package instructions and let it chill.
  2. Once the pudding is chilled, spoon a small amount into each serving dish to create the base for your eyeballs.
  3. Take each peeled grape and gently press a chocolate chip into the center of each grape to create the pupil.
  4. With a toothpick, draw red lines on the vanilla pudding base to represent the veins.
  5. Using a toothpick, mix a drop of blue food coloring with a drop of green food coloring and draw a small circle on each grape to create the iris.
  6. Place the prepared grapes on top of the vanilla pudding base.
  7. Chill the completed eyeball delights for at least 15 minutes before serving.
  8. Serve chilled and watch as your guests marvel at these deliciously spooky treats!
